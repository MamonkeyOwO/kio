❓ HOW TO PLAY
1. Pick a hero and travel through 3 Zones, each guarded by a Boss.
2. A word appears — pick the synonym (a word that means the same thing) to attack the enemy before time runs out.
3. Wrong or too slow = the enemy counterattacks your HP. Reach 0 HP and it's over.
4. Chain correct answers for a combo that boosts damage. Any miss resets it.
5. Defeat enemies to gain XP and level up, healing and strengthening your hero.
6. Hard mode uses scholar-level vocabulary and a shrinking timer. Good luck, Wordbinder.
