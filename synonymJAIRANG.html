❓ HOW TO PLAY
1. Pick a hero and set out on a 3-stage adventure, each stage a new biome with tougher foes.
2. A word appears — pick the synonym (a word that means the same thing) to attack the enemy before time runs out.
3. Wrong or too slow = the enemy counterattacks your HP. Reach 0 HP and it's over.
4. Chain correct answers for a combo that boosts damage. Any miss resets it.
5. Defeat every stage's Boss to earn coins and pick a permanent upgrade before moving on.
6. Vocabulary gets harder each stage — the Final Boss uses scholar-tier words and a razor-thin timer.
7. Beat the Final Boss to claim your spot on the Hall of Words podium.
